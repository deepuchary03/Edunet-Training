Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a field of computer science focused on creating machines that can perform tasks requiring human intelligence, such as learning, problem-solving, and decision-making. AI systems use data and advanced algorithms to recognize patterns, understand language, and make predictions or recommendations, allowing them to automate complex tasks and operate more autonomously. Applications range from personalizing recommendations and powering virtual assistants to enabling self-driving cars and assisting in medical diagnosis
